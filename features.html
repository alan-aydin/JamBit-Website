Play your own BitGame

BitGame is a fun feature that transforms your beat into a tiny pixelated game. You choose your sounds and rhythm, and BitGame turns them into levels, obstacles, and movement. With just a few taps, your music becomes a playable mini-game — a simple and creative way to bring your beats to life.
